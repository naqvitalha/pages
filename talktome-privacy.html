Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs the use of the voice-enabled app (the "App") and the data collected by the App.

The App does not upload or store audio files. However, the App may collect the following information:

  • Usage data: We may collect information about how the App is used, such as the time, frequency, and duration of use.
  • Device information: We may collect information about the device used to access the App, such as the device type, operating system, and IP address.
  • Interaction data: We may collect information about the user's interaction with the App, such as voice commands, responses, and queries.

Use of Information

We may use the information collected to:

  • Improve the App: We may use the information to improve the functionality and performance of the App.
  • Provide support: We may use the information to respond to user inquiries and support requests.

  • Comply with legal obligations: We may use the information to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Sharing of Information

We do not share or sell any user data collected by the App with third parties, except as required by law.

Security

We take appropriate measures to ensure the security of the information collected by the App, including encryption and access controls.
